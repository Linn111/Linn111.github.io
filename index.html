Welcome to Seminal Books

Seminal Books publishes books and articles from a small collection of authors. These books are usually offered in small volumes as POD (print-on-demand) using POD publishers such as Kindle Direct, IngramSpark or Lulu. Our intent is to make and market unique, quality books for nitch targeted markets. Our strategy is start small, meet the author's needs with the minimum fuss and get books in print. When sales reach a "tipping point" the books publishing can migrate as appropriate to larger, or more target-appropriate traditional publishers. Curently we have two target markets: 1) Swedish and Swedish-American genealogy and history and 2) Mental illness from patient's perspective, and three Authors and books coming in both markets.
